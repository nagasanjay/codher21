What is CODHER

Codher is a hackathon exclusively for women. We aim to provide a platform for women developers to create stunning tech products. Come and show your skills, win internships and grab exciting prizes!

Educational and Scientific Computing Society

ACM CEG organizes Codher every year and this is our 8th year. Learn more about us here.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it online or offline? Who are eligible to participate?

Codher is an online event. Only girls who are currently enrolled in college can participate.

How many members can be there in a team?

We allow a team of maximum of three members. We also allow lone wolves.

Is there any registration fee?

Yes, the registration fees is Rs 160 per team and for CEGians it is Rs 140 per team. The fees is lower if you come in through any referral.

How can I get a referral?

You can get a referral if you are associated with any of our Community Partners.

Can I start work before the hackathon?

No you are not supposed to work before the hackathon.

What are the perks of partcipating in Codher? Can I participate without a Computer Science background?

Yes! The hackathon will increase your knowledge and skill level. You will also be able to interact with mentors from various top companies and also socialize with the other participants
