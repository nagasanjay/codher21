What's CODHER

Codher is a platform for all talented women developers to come in and create a stunning tech product over the night. Come put your skills to good use and grab exciting prizes and internships!

The Largest Educational and Scientific Computing Society

"We strive to embark on the journey of learning amidst students by urging in them a passion for computer science through a tech-savvy attitude. We bring together computing educators and professionals in providing guidance to help people clear the clutter and achieve their goals".

Please take a look through our Frequently Asked Questions

Is it online or offline?

Codher is an online event

Who is eligible to participate?

Only girls who are currently enrolled in college can participate.

How many members can be there in a team?

We allow a team of maximum of three members. We also allow lone wolves.

Is there any registration fee?

Yes, the registration fees is Rs 160 per team. If you are a CEGian then the fees is Rs 140 per team. However the fees is lower if you come in through any referral.

How can I get a referral?

You can get a referral if you are associated with any of our Community Partners.

Can I participate without a Computer Science background?

Yes!

Can I start work before the hackathon?

No you are not supposed to work before the hackathon.

What are the perks of partcipating in Codher?

The hackathon will increase your knowledge and skill level. You will also be able to interact with mentors from various top companies and also socialize with the other participants
